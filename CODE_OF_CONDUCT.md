# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in optim-agent welcoming and harassment-free,
regardless of experience, identity, background, or technical preferences.

## Standards

Positive behavior includes being specific and constructive, respecting
different levels of experience, documenting evidence for technical claims,
and accepting responsibility for mistakes. Unacceptable behavior includes
harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sustained disruption, or sharing
another person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `admin@optim-agent.com`. Maintainers may
edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when behavior threatens a safe, productive community. Reports
will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.
